Analyzing the Portland City Charter Commission's Governmental Structural Reform Strategy

By Maggie Dapp · University of Portland, Class of 2026 · 2026

Abstract

This paper analyzes the recent reform to Portland city government by examining the problems the reform was meant to solve and assessing whether or not the reform was successful on its own terms. While it is difficult to come to clear conclusions so soon after the reforms were implemented, this paper finds that the reforms were partially successful at best, and more clarity is needed on governance challenges in Portland and how to measure governance progress.
