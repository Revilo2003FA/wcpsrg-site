IMAGES FOLDER — University of Portland campus photos
======================================================

The home page shows rotating photos as the hero background.
Put 4 photos in this folder, named exactly:

    campus-1.jpg
    campus-2.jpg
    campus-3.jpg
    campus-4.jpg

Tips:
- Use horizontal (landscape) photos for best results.
- Aim for 2000 x 1200 pixels or larger, under ~500 KB each.
- Save as JPG (smaller, faster loading).
- The photos will have a purple/dark overlay so text stays readable.

Where to get photos:
- Take your own photos of campus (best option — no copyright issues).
- Ask UP's communications office for official campus images you can
  use on a student research group site.
- For free stock photos of academic buildings, try Unsplash.com
  (search "campus" or "university"). All Unsplash photos are free to use.

Want more or fewer photos?
Open index.html and find the section marked "HERO". You can add or
remove <div class="bg-slide"> lines. Name your extra files
campus-5.jpg, campus-6.jpg, and so on.

Until you add real photos, the site falls back to a purple gradient
background — it still looks clean.
